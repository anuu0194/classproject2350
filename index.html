Welome to Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked country located in South Asia, nestled between China to the north and India to the south, east, and west. It is known for its diverse geography, ranging from the lowland Terai plains to the towering peaks of the Himalayas, including the world's highest, Mount Everest. Kathmandu, the capital and largest city, is a cultural and historical hub with a rich heritage of art, architecture, and religious sites.
